JIGARTHANDA-2(2023)

THIS IS THE FILM WHICH PERFECTLY FIT IN THE Martin Scorsese’S “THIS IS CINEMA” TEMPLATE.
PEAK PERFORMANCE BY THE PERFORMERS S.J.SURYA AND RAGHAVA LAWRANCE.
EVENTOUGH THE SCREENPLAY IN THE FIRST HALF IS SLOW IT STILL KEEPS THE AUDIENCE ENGAGING.
BUT THE SECOND HALF SERVES AS THE PEAK CINEMATIC MOMENTS .
PERFECT CHARACTER BUILDING AND PERFECT SCREENTIME FOR ALL THE CHARACTERS IS GIVEN.
MUSIC IS THE HEART OF THE FILM EVERY BGM PERFECTLY FITS IN THE SCENE.OVERALL A WORTH WATCH FILM…
